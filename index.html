With My Own Eyes is an ARPG in an Isometric world with fast paced combat and agility-demanding puzzles.

Will you break the cycle of brightness and darkness? Only your actions will help you on your path.

"This cave, again… Could’ve sworn I had it last time. I still don't know why the villagers attacked me though. I have to get up again. I have to get out of the darkness. I’ve got to make it to the end. There has to be a different way. There always is another!"
